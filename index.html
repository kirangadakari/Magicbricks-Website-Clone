Chennai Property Snapshot

A metropolitan city, Chennai is one of the most vital cities in India. Being a capital of Tamil Nadu, Chennai is not only the 31st largest urban area in the world but it is also the fourth- most popular metropolitan. A couple of months in Chennai are hot but rest of the year experiences a very pleasant and soothing temperature along with rains in between. Known for their liveliness and cleanliness, the beaches of Chennai are an ideal place to relax. Some of the famous beaches in Chennai are Elliot''s Beach, Marina Beach, Covelong Beach etc. The IT Park in Chennai is Asia''s largest Park and is also known as the Tidal Park. There are many job opportunities in Chennai in all the fields but especially for those who wish to work in BPOs or in the field of IT as Chennai is considered to be the second leading exporter of BPO services and information technology in India. Builders in Chennai have rightly understood the increase in demand for flats/apartments in Chennai and have been following the concept of affordable housing in Chennai to fulfil the housing needs of everyone. There is a wide variety of individual houses, flats, houses, residential plots for sale in Chennai. Other options like flats or rooms for rent in Chennai and PG in Chennai for males or females are also extensively available. Some of the famous residential areas in Chennai are Anna Nagar, Ashok Nagar, Gundy, KK Nagar, Kolathur, Medavakkam, Mylapore, Pallavaram, Velachery, East- Cost Road, Nungambakkam, Adyar etc.
